About us

The vision for Vietnam's Information Technology (IT) industry, outlined in 2015, aims to transform it into a dynamic, sustainable, and globally competitive sector. The focus is on meeting market demands, fostering a knowledge-based economy, and leading in information systems for digital sovereignty and security.

The Computer Science Bachelor's program, conducted in English, adopts a credit-based system. Aligned with renowned regional universities, it draws inspiration from institutions like The University of Queensland, The University of Adelaide, and the University of Technology Sydney.

Led by experienced faculty, including professors, associate professors, and PhDs, the program reflects their extensive teaching and research background. Many have contributed significantly to English-language international programs at the university since 2006.

Who can learn this program?

Students/candidates interested in computer science and information technology (IT), app and game development for mobile/web platforms, artificial intelligence (AI), and computer security and safety.
